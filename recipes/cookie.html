Cookie recipe

The best big batch cookie recipe: Award winning soft chocolate chip cookies

This recipe made perfectly soft chocolate chip cookies. The dough is made with two packets of instant pudding mix — an ingredient I had never seen anyone use in cookie dough — which seems to give the cookies a softer, more bakery-style texture. Think of these as the "soft-baked" style cookies that have a somewhat pliable, bendy texture.

Ingredients
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Baking soda
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Combine flour, baking soda and salt
  3. In large bowl, beat together sugar and margarine
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla extract until blended
  5. Gradually add flour mixture and beat just until blended. Stir in chocolate chips
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minsutes or until edges are golden
